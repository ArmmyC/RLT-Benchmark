## Paired Outcomes

Among tasks valid under both profiles:

- Paired-valid tasks: 11
- Conservative improved: 0
- Conservative matched: 10
- Conservative regressed: 1

Validity movement versus neutral:

- Gained validity: 1 task (`calculation`)
- Lost validity: 3 tasks (`adder`, `divider_4bit`, `fsm`)
- Same valid: 11 tasks
- Same invalid: 5 tasks

## Interpretation

`conservative_area` did not improve the paired generic-cell proxy score on any task. It matched most paired-valid tasks but regressed on `decoder_6bit`, and it lost net correctness-gated coverage relative to `neutral_baseline` by 2 records.

The small improvement in mean valid score for `conservative_area` is not enough to offset lower validity and lower all-sample score.

## Recommendation

Revise `conservative_area` before any larger run. Keep `neutral_baseline` as the default. Do not promote `conservative_area` to full RTL-OPT or private SiliconCraft work in its current form.

## Caveats

`generic_cells` is a public-flow area proxy, not final silicon area.

Power metrics are unavailable. This report makes no measured power-improvement claim.

Timing is not being optimized or claimed.

This is still a small public validation, not a SiliconCraft private benchmark.
