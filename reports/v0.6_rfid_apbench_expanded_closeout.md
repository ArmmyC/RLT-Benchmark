## Invalid Rows And Weak Tasks

Eight rows were invalid:

- 6 `empty_response`
- 1 `extraction_failure`
- 1 `synthesis_failure`

| task id | closeout observation |
| --- | --- |
| `ap_001_idle_counter` | 0/3 valid; all three completed generations returned empty responses. |
| `ap_010_retry_timeout_fsm` | 0/3 valid; all three completed generations returned empty responses. |
| `ap_004_crc_serial_parallel_tradeoff` | 2/3 valid; one functionally passing candidate failed synthesis under healthy tools. |
| `ap_005_fsm_controller_idle_activity` | 2/3 valid; one response lacked a complete required top module and failed extraction. |

The six empty rows are classified as model-output emptiness, not endpoint failures: generation completed 30/30 without request or transport failures. The extraction failure is consistent with an incomplete or malformed response, not demonstrated extractor damage. The synthesis failure is candidate-specific evidence under healthy tools; two other samples for that task and its known-good fixture synthesized successfully.

## Limitations

- Two tasks produced no valid model sample in this run.
- Eight of 30 attempts have no valid score, so the valid-only mean must not be treated as a whole-run result.
- The baseline covers one model and one neutral prompt profile; it does not establish a model comparison.
- Three samples per task provide bounded stability evidence, not an exhaustive distribution.
- Area uses generic synthesis cell counts, and activity uses a workload-specific VCD toggle-count proxy.
- The results are public/synthetic benchmark evidence, not production RFID/NFC or final silicon evidence.

## Benchmark Asset Decision

Keep benchmark assets unchanged. Reference validation passed 10/10, the `reference_copy` fixture passed 10/10, and 22 model rows passed all required gates. The invalid rows are sufficiently explained at a sanitized high level by model-output emptiness, one incomplete or malformed response, and one candidate-specific synthesis failure. No evidence supports changing tasks, prompts, references, testbenches, workloads, extraction, evaluation, metrics, or scoring.

## Fine-Tuning Readiness

Fine-tuning is not recommended solely from this evidence. RFID-APBench remains an evaluation benchmark, not a training dataset, and this single-model 30-sample run does not provide the data volume, diversity, governance, or causal evidence needed to justify training or adapter work.

## Next Milestone Direction

Recommend exactly one next direction: `v0.7_model_output_robustness_spec`.

That planning milestone should define sanitized failure-taxonomy and completion-reliability analysis for empty or incomplete model outputs. It must remain bounded and evidence-focused; this closeout does not implement a prompt experiment, model comparison, benchmark change, or model training.
