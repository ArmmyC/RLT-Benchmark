### Empty Responses

All six requests completed but returned no response text: three for the idle counter and three for the retry-timeout FSM. These rows are `model_output_empty`, not extraction failures. With 30/30 requests completed and no transport or request failures, endpoint instability is not supported.

### Extraction Failure

The FSM-controller sample did not contain a complete required top module. The sanitized evidence supports `likely_incomplete_or_malformed_response`. It does not establish extraction damage or an extractor defect, particularly because 23 responses extracted successfully and two other samples for the same task passed every gate.

### Synthesis Failure

The CRC sample extracted, compiled, passed functional simulation, and produced activity data, but failed synthesis under a healthy Yosys check. The supported high-level classification is `likely_candidate_synthesis_issue`. The sanitized evidence does not support an evaluator or benchmark-asset defect; two other samples for the task and the validated reference fixture synthesized successfully.
