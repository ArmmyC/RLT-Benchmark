# v0.4 Conservative Area v2 Closeout

## Decision

`neutral_baseline` remains the default baseline. `conservative_area_v2` is promoted as the v0.4 public RTL-OPT challenger profile, based on the completed public RTL-OPT adapter validation in Stage N. `low_area_low_power` and `conservative_area` remain held back.

This is a public RTL-OPT, correctness-gated, area-only closeout. It does not claim measured power, timing optimization, private-benchmark readiness, or final silicon PPA quality.

| Profile | Final status | Best public validation | Valid / samples | Mean valid score | Mean all-sample score | Decision rationale |
| --- | --- | --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- |
| `neutral_baseline` | default baseline | Stage N, 40 public RTL-OPT tasks | 25 / 40 | 1.163902 | 0.727439 | Stable control profile; remains the baseline for future comparisons. |
| `low_area_low_power` | held back | Stage J, 5 public RTL-OPT tasks | 2 / 5 | 1.104430 | 0.441772 | Lost validity versus neutral and did not improve paired score; not scaled. |
| `conservative_area` | held back | Stage L, 20 public RTL-OPT tasks | 12 / 20 | 1.124249 | 0.674549 | Safer than low-area intent, but lost net validity versus neutral and regressed `decoder_6bit`; not scaled. |
| `conservative_area_v2` | public RTL-OPT challenger | Stage N, 40 public RTL-OPT tasks | 30 / 40 | 1.125223 | 0.843917 | Higher validity and all-sample score than neutral, with no validity losses; keep separate from the default baseline. |

## Held-Back Profiles

`low_area_low_power` remains held back because its first matched v0.4 retest reduced correctness-gated coverage from 3/5 to 2/5, lost `add_sub`, and produced no paired improvement over neutral on the valid intersection. Prompt intent toward low area and low power is not sufficient evidence.

`conservative_area` remains held back because its 20-task validation underperformed neutral on validity, 12/20 versus 14/20, and lost validity on `adder`, `divider_4bit`, and `fsm`. It also regressed `decoder_6bit` among paired-valid tasks. The v2 prompt superseded it for public RTL-OPT challenger use.

## Final Recommendation

Use `neutral_baseline` as the default profile for future control runs. Use `conservative_area_v2` as the v0.4 public RTL-OPT challenger when comparing prompt-driven area-only behavior. Do not scale `low_area_low_power` or `conservative_area` without a new revision and fresh bounded evidence.

The next public step, if needed, is not to re-run Stage N but to use this closeout as the decision record for future full public RTL-OPT comparisons or for designing a separate private-benchmark gate.

## Caveats

- `generic_cells` is a public-flow area proxy, not final silicon area.
- Power metrics are unavailable in these v0.4 reports.
- Timing is not being optimized or claimed.
- This closeout covers public RTL-OPT only, not SiliconCraft private benchmarks.
- These results do not claim measured power improvement or final silicon PPA quality.
