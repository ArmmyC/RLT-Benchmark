## Empty-Response Interpretation

The four empty rows are successful, parsed endpoint responses rather than request failures. Each has `response_choice_count=1`, `response_content_present=false`, `response_character_count=0`, `finish_reason=length`, and `completion_tokens=4096`. Under the specified interpretation rules, this supports null response content normalized to empty text after reaching the completion-token limit. It does not support a zero-token completion, whitespace-only response, timeout, transport instability, HTTP failure, or response parse/schema defect.

Empty behavior remained concentrated and repeatable: `ap_010_retry_timeout_fsm` returned empty content for 3/3 samples, while `ap_001_idle_counter` returned empty content for 1/3 samples. The latter also produced two non-empty but functionally incorrect candidates.
